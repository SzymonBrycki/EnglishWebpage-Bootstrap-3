Social networks

Coursework, Information Technologies, KPSW

I use a lot of social networks, I do believe. Of course, my favorite one is Facebook. Thanks to that website, I'm able to communicate with my friends - most of whom are far away - I browse news, get exposed to new memeses and jokes etc. Yup, Facebook is really good!

I also have an account on Twitter, which is not so popular among Poles in my opinion. Twitter serves me mostly as a medium to get to know current political news and climate - and some of my favorite artists are available only on Twitter, which is an additional incentive to use this website.

I also have a profile on LinkedIn. For some reasons I do not wanna discuss here I have never worked, but if I will be able one day - today I am not - it will be probably thanks to my account on LI.

An additional thing - I have a userpage on LibraryThing, which is basically Goodreads, but more professional. My book collection is nowadays in two separate flats in two different cities, but if I will get them in one place I am gonna start cataloguing them. I can;t wait to meet book lovers who have the same literary taste as I do! What a pleasure it is gonna be!
